Homebuying 101: How to Buy a Home

Ten-hour course designed for any first-time homebuyer to get familiar with the home buying process from start to end

  1. How is it useful to me?

    • Required if you are applying for financial assistance from the City.

    • You will learn about mortgage process, managing debt, shopping for a home, and legal concerns related to homebuying

    • Required to gain access to various city programs

    2. Get More Information

  2. What is the class fee?

    • $25 for Boston residents

    3. Check the schedule for upcoming classes

Affordable Home Opportunities

  1. How is it useful to me?

    • It's an opportunity to purchase an affordable home in one of Boston’s neighborhoods.

    2. Get More Information

  2. Am I eligible for this?

    You might be eligible if

    • Your household income is less than $113,800

    • You are first time home buyer

    3. Check your eligibility

  3. How to apply?

    • First check if you are eligible to participate in this program

    • Apply to the Neighborhood Homes Initiative

    • Qualified applicants will be automatically entered into every lottery for which they are qualified and meet the income limits

    4. Download the NHI application

Financial Assistance Program

  1. How is it useful to me?

    • Offers up to 3% of the purchase price towards down payment

    • Offers closing costs for 1-2 unit properties and condominiums

    • 5% of purchase price for 3-unit properties

    • Benefits including zero interest, deferred payments, 10-year term loan

    2. Get More Information

  2. Am I eligible for this?

    You might be eligible if

    • Your household assets are not more than $75,000

    • Your credit score is at least 660

    • You have completed Boston Homebuying 101 and Homeowner 201 classes

    3. Check your eligibility

    • You can find all the step wise information about the application process by visiting the following link

    4. Apply for this program
